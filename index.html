The DeepGuard Well Integrity Platform

A single pane of glass for wells: MAASP calculations, annulus surveillance, barrier status, and integrity tasks – ready to plug in your existing data.

Annulus & MAASP Engine

Define annuli (A/B/C/D), critical points, fluid gradients and limits. DeepGuard computes MAASP, utilisation and status (GREEN/AMBER/RED) using standardised logic you control.

  • Multi-point MAASP calculation per annulus
  • Configurable safety factors & triggers
  • Cached results exposed via API

Barrier & Envelope Management

Map all well barrier elements – packer, SSSV, valves, tree, cement – and see primary/secondary envelopes with live integrity status.

  • Barrier element registry
  • Test results & leak acceptance
  • Envelope visualisation (future D3)

Tasks, Alerts & Governance

Turn measurements into action: DeepGuard generates integrity review tasks, test reminders, and escalations when annuli approach MAASP.

  • Rule-based alerting (90% / 100% MAASP)
  • Per-well task list & audit trail
  • ISO-style reporting (future enhancement)

Beautiful Well Integrity Diagrams

DeepGuard is designed to render interactive well schematics using D3.js – casings, tubing, cement, annuli and barrier elements. The diagrams update as MAASP and integrity status change.

What the diagrams will show

  • All tubular strings with depths and sizes
  • Cement tops, zonal isolation, and weak formations
  • Annuli with live pressure & MAASP utilisation colours
  • Barrier elements: packer, SSSV, wellhead, X-mas tree valves
  • Hover-based tooltips: test history, status, anomaly notes

The frontend D3 engine will consume JSON from an endpoint (e.g. /wells/{id}/schematic) and render the complete vertical well profile.

Product Roadmap

DeepGuard starts with deterministic calculations and clear dashboards. AI/ML is reserved for a defined future phase once operators trust the fundamentals.

Now

Core Integrity Platform

  • Well & annulus registry
  • MAASP & utilisation calculations
  • Annulus status (GREEN/AMBER/RED)
  • Task creation & reminders
  • API-first backend for integration
Next

D3-Based Well Schematic

  • Interactive vertical schematics
  • Barrier envelopes & leak paths
  • Pressure/temperature overlay
  • Export to PDF/PNG for reports
Future

AI/ML Predictive Integrity (Future State)

  • Annulus pressure escalation prediction
  • Casing & tubing failure probability
  • Optimised test frequencies & workovers
  • Natural language “Integrity Copilot” assistant

These capabilities will be introduced cautiously, keeping traceability and deterministic calculations at the core of the system.
